I received my PhD in 2013 from Penn State University where I studied mosquito feeding behavior and nutritional requirements in the context of understanding the spread of mosquito-borne diseases. I enjoy experimenting and building tools that can make our world a healthier and less mosquito-ey place. I like to work on inter-disciplinary teams and use data-driven approaches to problem-solving. I'm motivated by a mission to leave the world better than I've found it.
